Nexus Media pride ourselves in being a small agile team that pulls off the exceptional. Reach out to see how our team can do that for your brand too.

We started in 2005 as a pop-culture publication run out of a Randburg living room, covering gaming, comics and tech for South African readers who were not being served anywhere else.

The publication became a community. The community became an audience. The audience became a network. And along the way, we figured out how to help brands plug into all of it, without making it weird.

Small team. We like it that way. Fewer clients, better work, more sleep for everyone.
